# Privacy Policy for Private DNS Extension

Last updated: October 12, 2025

## Data Collection
- We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data
- All DNS queries are processed through your own DNS server
- No browsing history is collected or transmitted
- Settings are stored locally in your browser only

## Third-Party Services
- DNS queries are sent to your configured DNS server: yogvidwankhede.duckdns.org
- No third-party analytics or tracking services are used

## Data Security
- All DNS queries use encrypted HTTPS (DNS-over-HTTPS)
- Your browsing data never leaves your device except for DNS resolution
